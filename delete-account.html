Delete Your Account

You can delete your Tapara account at any time. Here’s how.

Delete from the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the Tapara app:

  1. Open the Tapara app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion.

Deletion is immediate and permanent. Once confirmed, your account cannot be recovered.

No app access?

If you can no longer access the app, you can request deletion by email:

  1. Email support@tapara.co.nz with the subject line “Account Deletion Request”.
  2. Include the email address registered to your Tapara account in the body of the email.

Email deletion requests are processed within 7 days.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account and login credentials.
  • Personal profile information (name, email).
  • Transaction and loyalty history.
  • Any linked bank connection data.

Data retention

Certain records required for legal and financial compliance may be retained for up to 7 years in accordance with New Zealand law. These records are not accessible to you or to merchants and are held solely to meet our regulatory obligations.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at support@tapara.co.nz.
