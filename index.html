Four steps. No terminal.

The entire interaction — recognition, order, payment — happens before a word is exchanged about money.

1

Customer taps

Phone touches the tapara NFC pad on the counter. A small branded square — no power, no lease.

2

Business sees who

The tapara app shows the customer's name, tier, and usual order — Coffee Lover · 12 visits — before they speak.

3

Payment request sent

The customer's phone buzzes. Lock screen shows the merchant name and amount. Face ID approves it.

4

Done

The customer confirms in their bank app. Money moves directly — near-zero fees, no interchange, no scheme fee, no acquirer margin.
