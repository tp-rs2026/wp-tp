Complaints Process

Tapara Limited (FSP1012381) is registered on the New Zealand Financial Service Providers Register and is a member of the Financial Dispute Resolution Service (FDRS).

We are committed to resolving any complaints promptly and fairly. This page explains how to make a complaint and what to expect.

How to Make a Complaint

If you have a complaint about Tapara’s services, please contact us:

Email: support@tapara.co.nz

When submitting a complaint, please include:

  • Your name and the email address associated with your Tapara account
  • A description of the issue
  • The date(s) of the relevant transaction(s), if applicable
  • What outcome you are seeking

Our Internal Process

  1. Acknowledgement: We will acknowledge your complaint within 5 business days of receiving it.
  2. Investigation: We will investigate the matter and keep you informed of progress.
  3. Resolution: We aim to resolve complaints within 40 business days. If we need more time, we will let you know and explain why.
  4. Outcome: We will provide you with a written response explaining our findings and any action we propose to take.

If You Are Not Satisfied

If we are unable to resolve your complaint, or you are not satisfied with our response, you may refer it to the Financial Dispute Resolution Service (FDRS).

The FDRS is a free, independent service that helps resolve disputes between consumers and financial service providers.

How the FDRS Process Works

  1. Contact FDRS: Submit your complaint online or call them. They will guide you through the process.
  2. Provider resolution opportunity: Your financial service provider (Tapara) has an opportunity to work with you to resolve the issue.
  3. Mediation: If the issue is not resolved, FDRS will work with both parties to reach an agreement. This may include mediation.
  4. Adjudication: If agreement cannot be reached, a specialist adjudicator will investigate and make a decision that is binding on Tapara.

FDRS Contact Details

  • Website: fdrs.org.nz
  • Phone: 0508 337 337
  • Online complaints: fdrs.org.nz/complaints

The FDRS service is free for consumers. Most complaints are resolved within 2 months.

Complaints About Merchants

If your complaint is about the goods or services provided by a merchant (rather than the Tapara platform itself), you should contact the merchant directly. Tapara facilitates payments and loyalty programs but is not responsible for the goods or services sold by merchants.

If a merchant does not resolve your complaint satisfactorily, you may also seek assistance from:

  • Consumer NZ: consumer.org.nz
  • Disputes Tribunal: For claims up to $30,000 — disputestribunal.govt.nz

Privacy Complaints

If your complaint relates to how we handle your personal information, you may also contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner:

  • Website: privacy.org.nz
